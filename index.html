Think Tank

The quality of output depends on the quality of input. This module helps you think in frameworks — not just "what to buy" but "how to think about buying." Long-term wealth is built on mental models.

🧠 Mental Models for Wealth
COMPOUNDING
The 8th Wonder — Time × Rate
₹10,000 at 15% CAGR = ₹4.04 Lakh in 25 years. The rule: never interrupt compounding unnecessarily. Each withdrawal resets the clock. Your biggest wealth weapon at 19 is time — not stock picks.
OPPORTUNITY COST
Every rupee has an alternative
Spending ₹60,000 on a gaming PC isn't ₹60K — it's ₹24.4 Lakh in 25 years at 15% CAGR. That doesn't mean never buy — it means be conscious of what you're trading.
ASYMMETRY
Seek asymmetric bets
Look for situations where downside is capped but upside is unlimited. A ₹5,000 course that makes you 10x more productive is asymmetric. A fixed deposit at 7% is not. Most retail investors miss this entirely.
SECOND ORDER
Think beyond the obvious
First-order: "RBI cuts rates → buy bonds." Second-order: "Rate cut means economy expanding → buy cyclicals." Third-order: "Expansion means more demand for power → buy infra." Most people stop at first order.
